# A Study on Developing the Lifestyle Modification Program for Adolescents

## Overview
This repository accompanies my Undergraduate Thesis: *A Study on Developing the Lifestyle Modification Program for Adolescents*  
Published in the International Journal of Indian Psychology (IJIP), 2023.  
Read the full paper here: https://ijip.in/articles/lifestyle-modification-program

The study investigates how lifestyle modification programs can help adolescents manage everyday distress and improve overall well-being.

---

## Objectives
- To design and evaluate a lifestyle modification program tailored for adolescents.
- To address everyday distress caused by academic, social, and developmental challenges.
- To provide practical strategies for promoting mental health and resilience.

---

## Methodology
- **Design:** Mixed-methods approach (qualitative + quantitative).
- **Participants:** Adolescent student population.
- **Focus:** Identifying stressors and evaluating program effectiveness.
- **Analysis:** Statistical and thematic analysis to assess outcomes.

---

## Key Findings
- Adolescents reported significant everyday distress linked to academic and social pressures.
- Participants expressed strong interest in structured lifestyle modification programs.
- Findings highlight the importance of supportive interventions for adolescent mental health.
